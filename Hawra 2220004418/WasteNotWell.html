Zero Hunger, Zero Waste - Utilizing Resources and Saving Food

this website is a powerful resource to help combat food waste and reduce resource consumption. It provides information on how to store food properly, recipes for leftovers, ways to donate illegal food items, and advice to foster mindful eating and create sustainable habits. Easily accessible, this website helps support the UN sustainability goals of Zero Hunger, Responsible Consumption and Production, and Life Below Water.
